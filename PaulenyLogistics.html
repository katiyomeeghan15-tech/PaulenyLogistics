About Us

Pauleny Logistics was incorporated on 3 February 2020. We provide fuel transportation both locally and internationally. Our mission is to provide excellent and reliable logistics solutions for our customers and it is a trusted logistics company specializing in bulk transportation of fuel across Zimbabwe. Our commitment is to provide safe, efficient, and reliable logistics solutions for all our clients. We take pride in being a vital part of the fuel distribution network in the region.

The business is owned and managed by Mr. Paul Katiyo

Our VisionTo be the most preferred reliable and convenient fuel transporter within and outside the country. We aim to provide excellent services with our well serviced and maintained fleet of tankers.

Our Services

We transport medium to large scale motor and industrial fuels and oils. Pauleny Logistics currently operates with tankers with a capacity to carry 45,0001trs. We continue to invest in our fleet to ensure we have the most efficient and dependable tankers to promote timely deliveries in time. We transport bulk fuels and oils in all SADC countries.

The Commercial Business line in charge of supplying medium to large scale clients with both motor and industrial fuels. We have cultivated a commercial customer base over the years and intend to become the most preferred commercial transporter of fuels in Zimbabwe.

  • Bulk fuel transportation
  • Efficient and timely fuel delivery
  • Safe handling and logistics of petroleum products
  • Nationwide logistics solutions
  • Customized delivery schedules
  • Modern fleet and safety-first approach
